Developers and contributors of JuliaDynamics

This page has a two-fold goal: First, to list the people that are involved with the various packages of JuliaDynamics, and convey their role in the package governance. Second, to clearly acknowledge significant contributions, as GitHub's automated "Contributions" tab is quite often missleading.

The organization as a whole is led by George Datseris.

DynamicalSystems.jl

(includes sub-packages: ChaosTools.jl, DynamicalSystemsBase.jl)

Lead developers: George Datseris.

Significant contributions:

  • Alexandre Wagemakers: Basins of attraction, final state sensitivity.
  • Hauke K. Kraemer: delay coordinates embedding, both traditional as well as unified.
  • Anton P. Braun: several fractal dimension estimators.
  • Inga Kottlarz: Takens' best estimator for fractal dimension.
  • Anshul Singhvi: Automatic period estimation.
  • oneil: Performance improvement of histogram estimation.
  • Elliot Fosong: Predictability classification method.
  • Allen Hill: Delay time estimation methods.
  • Jonas Isensee: Cao's method for embedding dimension and other delay time methods.
  • Sebastian Micluța-Câmpeanu: Poincare section and Lyapunov exponents related contributions.
